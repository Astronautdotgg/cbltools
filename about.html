About CBL Tools

The Crypto Blacklist is a list maintained by Astronaut for the express goal of allowing other communities/projects to alleviate the stress of determining whether potential new members harbor malicious intent. This list currently supports only Discord, but is currently under development to support multiple networks. We have also created a Discord bot (Cosmo) that can help you interact with the blacklist, among other cryptocurrency-related functions.
